Welcome to

Neighbors Pizza and Beer Garden

At Neighbors, we take a unique approach to preparing your food. We use the highest quality products and everything is prepared fresh. This means fresh dough, fresh cheese, fresh tomatoes, and more. We would love to see you and your family enjoying our space with delicious pizza and beer.

We are not fast food. We are fresh food. We love our food like we love our family.

At Neighbors, quality is everything. In a world where a drive-thru is the norm and food is often over-processed, prepackaged and always frozen, Neighbors sets itself apart by doing things the old fashioned way. We use the same sourdough start for all of our pizzas. We offer neapolitan style and Detroit style sourdough pizza with a variety of fresh toppings.
